Protocols & methodology

Regulator-compliant measurements

Our campaigns strictly follow regulator technical specifications with traceable protocols and certified equipment.

Field methodology

  • Stratified routes: dense urban, peri-urban, rural, road corridors
  • Critical time slots: 8-10 AM (morning peak), 12-2 PM (midday), 5-7 PM (evening peak)
  • Sampling: every 2-5 s depending on vehicle speed
  • Technologies covered: 2G (GSM/GPRS/EDGE), 3G (UMTS/HSPA+), 4G (LTE/LTE-A), 5G NSA/SA

Certified equipment

  • Multi-operator scanners: Keysight/TEMS/Nemo
  • Calibrated test smartphones (reference + DUT)
  • Dedicated FTP/HTTP/streaming test servers
  • High-precision GPS (<5 m accuracy)

KPIs by service

Layer-by-layer scoring

Granular evaluation following regulator weighting: Voice 40%, Data 50%, SMS 10%.

VOICE service (40% weight)

  • Accessibility: Call Setup Success Rate ≥98%
  • Retention: Call Drop Rate ≤2%
  • Integrity: audio MOS (POLQA/PESQ) ≥3.5/5
  • Setup time: <6 s (2G/3G), <3 s (4G/5G)

DATA service (50% weight)

  • RF accessibility: PDP/PDN activation ≥98%
  • RF retention: uninterrupted sessions ≥97%
  • Application accessibility: HTTP/FTP success ≥95%
  • Throughput: 4G DL ≥15 Mbps urban, UL ≥5 Mbps
  • Latency: ping <50 ms (4G), <20 ms (5G)

SMS service (10% weight)

  • Success rate: send/receive ≥98%
  • End-to-end delay: <10 seconds
  • Overall scoring: 0-100 normalisation against regulator thresholds
  • Heatmaps: KPI and area geolocation
